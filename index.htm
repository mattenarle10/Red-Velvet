Red Velvet

Red Velvet is a South Korean girl group formed and managed by SM Entertainment. They originally debuted on August 1, 2014 with the single "Happiness" with the four-member line-up of Irene, Seulgi, Wendy and Joy. A fifth member, Yeri, joined the group in March 2015, following the release of their first mini album, Ice Cream Cake.

Albums

The Red

The Red in September 2015, with the lead single "Dumb Dumb". The album topped various music charts, including the Billboard World Albums Charts, while "Dumb Dumb" became their first single to surpass a million downloads.

The Perfect Red Velvet

A truly consistent pop album is hard to come by, but Red Velvet have made a few of them to date -- and Perfect Velvet may be their crowning jewel.

The Reve Festival: Finale

The album contains a total of sixteen tracks, with twelve of which were taken from the group's first two installations from the trilogy in the same year. The lead single "Psycho" appeared on several year-end music lists as one of the best K-pop releases of 2019.

Summer Magic

Summer Magic is the second special extended play and the seventh overall by South Korean girl group Red Velvet, marketed as their special "summer" EP following The Red Summer, which was released in July 2017.

Ice Cream Cake

Ice Cream Cake marks the first release from Red Velvet to contain both "Red" and "Velvet" compositions, The extended play starts with the same-name track "Ice Cream Cake", a "punchy, sugary pop confection" that consists elements from dance-rock, bubblegum-pop and drum and bass.

Subunit & Solo

Irene & Seulgi: Monster

Irene & Seulgi is the first sub-unit of South Korean girl group Red Velvet, formed by SM Entertainment in 2020. It is composed of members Irene and Seulgi. The duo debuted on July 6, 2020, with the extended play Monster.

Wendy: Like Water

Like Water is the debut extended play by South Korean singer Wendy. The EP was released on April 5, 2021, by SM Entertainment and consists of five tracks, including the two title tracks, "When This Rain Stops" and "Like Water".

Joy: Hello

Hello is the debut extended play by Red Velvet Joy. It was released on May 31, 2021. The EP consists of six new remakes of famous songs from the 1990s and 2000s, including the title song "Hello" and pre-released single "Je T'aime".
